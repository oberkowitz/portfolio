About

Oren Berkowitz is a California-born and Berlin-based musician, producer, DJ, performer and sound engineer. His omnivorous taste for all types of music stems from a diverse range of influences ranging from the Talking Heads, Suzanne Ciani, Hiroshi Yoshimura, LCD Soundsystem, ESG, and countless others. He feels at home in the in-between DIY and underground spaces in the cracks of the city.
