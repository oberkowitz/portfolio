Inattention

Halffish

February 2016 • Berlin, Germany • Electronic

About

Inattention is an early Halffish EP that introduces a minimal yet expressive approach to electronic production. The release moves through long-form compositions with detailed textures and subtle shifts in rhythm and harmony.

Across four tracks, the project develops a meditative and immersive atmosphere that highlights restraint, space, and tonal contrast while preserving a strong sense of groove.
