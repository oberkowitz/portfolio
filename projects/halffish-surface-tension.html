Surface Tension

Halffish

December 2019 • Berlin, Germany • Electronic

About

Surface Tension is an EP by Halffish, the electronic music project of Oren Berkowitz. The release moves through spacious, patient arrangements that sit between deep house and downtempo electronics, emphasizing texture, momentum, and atmosphere.

Released in December 2019, the record presents five long-form tracks that build gradually and reward close listening. The project reflects a club-informed sensibility while staying introspective and immersive.
