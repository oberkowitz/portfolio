EDGE001 - Spaceport Lounge Music

Vitling / Edge Network

April 2021 • Berlin, Germany • House / Electronic

About

The first release on Edge Network, Spaceport Lounge Music features original tracks by Vitling with remixes by Halffish and DJ Sterni, exploring synth-heavy house textures and cosmic club moods.
