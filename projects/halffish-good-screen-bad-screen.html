Good Screen, Bad Screen

Halffish

December 2020 • Berlin, Germany • Electronic

About

Good Screen, Bad Screen is an EP by Halffish centered on the contradiction of living in screens across both work and creative life. The record was written for live performance and explores the push and pull between digital immersion and embodied experience.

The release combines driving rhythmic structures with detailed sound design and club-informed arrangements, while reflecting on burnout, repetition, and the struggle for focus inside contemporary online life.
