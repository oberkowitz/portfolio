September 2025 • Berlin, Germany • Punk

About

Parasocialist is a California-born and Berlin-based musical project by Oren Berkowitz. As a musician, producer, DJ, performer and sound engineer, Berkowitz draws from a diverse range of influences including Talking Heads, Suzanne Ciani, Hiroshi Yoshimura, LCD Soundsystem, ESG, and countless others. The project finds its home in the DIY and underground spaces in the cracks of the city.

Rejoice! EP

Weaving sardonic wit and playful defiance, Rejoice! confronts the absurdities and paradoxes of contemporary society and politics. The songs contemplate everything from escapism, the job market, foraging, burnout, the passage of time and environmental collapse while searching for silver linings in dark times.

With an iterative process based on creation and refinement, the EP layers blaring guitars, brash drums, and rumbling bass with a staccato vocal style. All instrumental parts and lead vocals were written and performed by the artist. The EP was written and recorded in Berlin's historic Funkhaus and the artist's home studio between January and May 2025.
